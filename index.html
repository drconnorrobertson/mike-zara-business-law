About the Attorney

Michael A. Zara, Esq.

With nearly two decades of legal experience and a background in accounting, Mike Zara brings a uniquely practical perspective to business law. He earned his J.D. from the University of Denver Sturm College of Law and holds a B.S. in Accounting from Arizona State University.

Licensed to practice in Colorado, Arizona, Georgia, and North Carolina, Mike serves clients nationwide through strategic partnerships and pro hac vice admissions. Every client receives his direct phone number because business decisions do not wait for office hours.
