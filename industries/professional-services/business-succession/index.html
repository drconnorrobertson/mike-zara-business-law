Professional Services Firms + Business Succession

Professional services firm succession planning is uniquely complex because firm value is often closely tied to personal client relationships, professional reputations, and the specialized expertise of individual partners. Unlike product businesses, professional services firms cannot simply transfer ownership without carefully managing the transition of client trust and professional relationships.

We help professional services firm owners develop and implement succession strategies that include phased retirement transitions with defined client introduction timelines, book-of-business valuation methodologies appropriate for professional services, buyout structures funded through firm earnings, retained interest arrangements, or external financing, next-generation partner development and equity participation plans, and client notification and transition protocols that protect retention rates.
