Who We Serve

Legal Counsel for Every Stage of Business

Whether you are launching your first venture or managing a portfolio of companies, Zara Business Law provides the strategic legal counsel you need.

Startups & Emerging Businesses

Starting a business means making critical legal decisions from day one. We help founders choose the right entity structure, draft founder agreements, protect intellectual property, create initial contracts, and establish the corporate governance framework that will support long-term growth.

Common services: Entity formation, founder agreements, IP protection, initial contract templates, equity structures, advisory board formation.

Small Businesses

As your business grows, so do your legal needs. From employment policies and vendor contracts to lease negotiations and dispute resolution, we serve as outside general counsel for small businesses that need reliable, accessible legal support without the overhead of an in-house team.

Common services: Contract review, employment policies, lease negotiations, dispute resolution, compliance, vendor agreements.

Get Started

Mid-Market Companies

Mid-market businesses face complex legal challenges as they scale operations, enter new markets, pursue acquisitions, or prepare for exit. We provide the sophisticated legal counsel these companies need while maintaining the personal, direct access that larger firms often cannot offer.

Common services: M&A advisory, governance restructuring, multi-state compliance, succession planning, strategic partnerships.

Get Started

Franchise Owners & Franchisors

The franchise model comes with unique legal requirements at both the franchisor and franchisee level. We help franchise owners navigate FDD review, franchise agreement negotiation, multi-unit expansion, compliance, transfers, and disputes with clarity and confidence.

Common services: FDD review, franchise agreement negotiation, multi-unit planning, compliance, resales and transfers, dispute resolution.
